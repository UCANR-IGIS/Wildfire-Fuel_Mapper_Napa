WILDFIRE FUEL TREATMENT REPORT

Step 1: Select Your Report Type

Select 'Single Parcel' for a one parcel report with maps (very small parcels are not available), Multi Parcel for a multiple parcel report with maps, and Watershed/FSC for a report with maps for an entire watershed.

Step 2: Select Your Parcel(s)

Add the Address or APN of the parcel of interest in the search bar OR Navigate to the parcel (use the +/- buttons to zoom, and click and drag to move the map), select your parcel

Step 3: Download Your Map

Click on the download icon to get your custom map

Step 4: Get Information

Go to the Resources Page to get information on Service Providers and Funding Opportunities for your fuels management project.

Based on your selected parcels, you will receive a Microsoft word report that includes information on your parcels in the context of fuels and wildfire, with associated PDF maps that show fuels, vegetation, and other landscape characteristics.
