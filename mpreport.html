WILDFIRE FUEL TREATMENT REPORT

Step 1: Select Your Parcel(s)

Navigate to your parcel on the map and click to select or use the search bar to select by address or APN. Multiple parcels may be selected.

Reset Parcel Selection

Step 2: Select Your Maps

Select the maps you would like for the report.

Step 3: Generate A Fuel Treatment Report


Based on your planned treatment areas, you can create a PDF report summarizing available datasets, including fuel types, associated treatment costs, vegetation, topography, and additional environmental and jurisdictional information. This report will have much of the information needed to apply for fuels reduction grants.

Click the button to generate your PDF report. You will receive an email with a link to the PDF within 5-10 minutes.
