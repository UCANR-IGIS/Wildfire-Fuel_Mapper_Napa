RESOURCES & DATA

In addition to custom maps that can help users visualize their landscape and fuels on their property, there are additional resources available: contact information for specialists and experts, funding opportunities to help support fuel management projects, and educational materials related to vegetation management and fire preparedness.

Data
Access and download all Wildfire Fuel Mapper data.

Specialists
Before you start any fuel management, you’ll need to work with a specialist to make a plan that fits your property’s needs. Find specialists and experts to help you plan your fuel management project.

Funding
Fuel Management can be expensive, but there are many funding programs to help you cover the costs. Find funding opportunities to help support your fuel management project.

Learn
There are many regional and educational resources to help you manage fuels and prepare for fire. Explore fire preparedness education and fuel management resources.
