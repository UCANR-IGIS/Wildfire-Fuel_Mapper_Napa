CUSTOM MAPS

The Wildfire Fuel Mapper User Manual is designed to help users access and understand their parcel and watershed reports, in addition to providing information on steps to planning a fuel management project. To download the Wildfire Fuel Mapper User Manual, please click here.

How to create your custom map

Follow the steps below to create custom maps for your property (parcel) or watershed. The report you generate will include maps and informational summaries of landscape elements such as vegetation and fuels to help you assess fire hazards for your selected parcel or watershed.

  1. Download a parcel or watershed report by clicking on either the parcel map or watershed map below. *Note that only parcels greater than 3 acres are available.*
  2. Then, navigate to your parcel or watershed on the map, or using the search button on the upper left where you can type an address
  3. When you have found your parcel or watershed, click it to download your report and maps.
